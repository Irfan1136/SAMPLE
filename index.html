🩸 About SEVAGAN

Your Blood Donor Partner

Our Mission

SEVAGAN is a life-saving digital platform built to connect voluntary blood donors with those in urgent need. Whether it's a medical emergency, surgery, or a rare blood type requirement – SEVAGAN bridges the gap between donors and patients with just a few taps.

Our mission is simple: To make blood donation faster, easier, and accessible for everyone.

We believe that every drop counts and that technology can make life-saving connections happen faster than ever before.

🔐 Key Features

✅ Secure OTP-based Login

Via Email or Mobile for safe access

📍 Smart Search

By Blood Group, City & Pincode

📒 Safe Donor Records

Storage in Excel/Database format

🛎️ Instant Alerts

Blood Request notifications
